Who we are

At Seven Hills, not only do we have state-of-the-art, well maintained skydiving equipment, but we have extremely well qualified instructors and coaches that put safety above all else. Don't take our word for it: We have been in business for over 60 years with a consistent safety record. At Seven Hills, you're in the best hands.

Skydiving in Appleton, WI

Seven Hills is Appleton's closest skydiving center. We're only one and a half hours away from Appleton. A simple, quick drive down on I-41 and US-151 for all Appletonians!

Why jump with Seven Hills Skydivers

Seven Hills Skydivers is a nonprofit, volunteer owned and operated skydiving center located Northeast of Madison, WI in business for more than 60 years. We have highly qualified instructors and coaches, and have safety record second to none. To learn more about us, click here.

If you're interested in making a Tandem skydive or going solo, we have the expertise to provide a safe, fun experience that you will never forget.

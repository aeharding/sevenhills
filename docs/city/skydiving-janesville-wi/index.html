Why jump with Seven Hills Skydivers

Seven Hills is a nonprofit, volunteer owned and operated skydiving center located Northeast of Madison, WI. We've been in business for over 55 years! We have a very experienced staff, and we have an excellent safety record. If you want to learn a bit more about us, click here.

We offer both Tandem skydiving and solo skydiving, and we have the expertise for an unforgettable, safe, fun experience.
